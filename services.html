What We Offer

Backlog Scanning

Our backlog scanning service is designed to efficiently convert large volumes of physical files into digital records. We use high-speed, high-capacity scanners like the Ricoh fi-8170 to process documents quickly and securely.

Whether you are digitizing years of archived files or clearing storage space, we ensure precise, organized, and accessible digital formats that streamline your operations.

EDMS Installation

We provide end-to-end Electronic Document Management System (EDMS) setup and configuration. Our solutions help you manage, track, and securely store digital documents.

Our document capture systems ensure a seamless transition from paper to digital with user-friendly interfaces that make document retrieval fast and easy.

Document Destruction

We offer secure document destruction services for sensitive or obsolete records. Our process ensures that confidential information is permanently destroyed, reducing the risk of data breaches.

We provide verifiable shredding and disposal in line with professional and legal standards for privacy protection.

File Registry Arrangement

Our team specializes in reorganizing and indexing your physical records to improve accessibility and management. We offer systematic file registry setups that simplify tracking, retrieval, and archiving.

We help you build or modernize your registry with clear labeling and digital indexing for faster operations.
